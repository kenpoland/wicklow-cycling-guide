🚴‍♂️ Wicklow Cycling Route Overview

📍 Bray Seafront

Start your ride by the Irish Sea with views of Bray Head and the Victorian promenade.

📍 Killruddery House

Historic estate home of the Earls of Meath. Rumors speak of a lady in grey in the gardens.

📍 Powerscourt Estate & Waterfall

Explore Ireland’s tallest waterfall and enchanted glens guarded by a water spirit.

📍 Lough Tay

This “Guinness Lake” is steeped in legend—some believe it’s a fairy portal into another world.

📍 Glendalough

Ancient monastic site founded by St. Kevin. Blackbirds, milk-giving stones, and immortal geese await 🕊️
