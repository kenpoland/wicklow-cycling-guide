📸 Photo Checklist

Moments worth capturing on your Wicklow ride

🏞 Route | ✍️ Journal | 📸 Checklist | 🌌 Fairy Lore

Your Snapshot Quest

  • ☐ Bray Head view from the beachside promenade
  • ☐ The ornate gardens or front façade of Killruddery House
  • ☐ Powerscourt Waterfall framed by forest (bonus: long exposure!)
  • ☐ Enniskerry’s village square with your bike in the frame
  • ☐ Sheep near Sally Gap (they’re natural models!) 🐑
  • ☐ Wide-angle shot of Lough Tay – "The Guinness Lake" 🍺
  • ☐ Castle Kevin ruins under ivy and shadow
  • ☐ Round Tower in Glendalough at golden hour or misty dawn
  • ☐ A fairy light or mystery shadow in the woods 🌌
  • ☐ Your bike beside a mossy wall, gate, or waterfall
  • ☐ Your ride crew mid-climb or mid-laugh 🚴‍♂️🚴‍♀️
  • ☐ A trail sign or marker you didn’t expect
  • ☐ That one photo that captures what the ride meant to you ❤️

Tip: Mark photos with 📷 after you’ve taken them—or draw them later in your journal if your camera missed the moment.
